Biodiversity at ISAK

Exploring the diversity of soil type and plant life in the ISAK campus

Introduction

This website is dedicated to our investigation into the relationship between soil type and the biodiversity of plant life. Our project aims to explore 5 soil types around campus, count different species of plants and compare number of species in different areas. On this website, you can learn more about our research project, including our methods, results, and conclusions.

Our goal

Investigate the relationship between soil type and the biodiversity of plant life around campus.

Outline

  • Investigate 5 soil types around campus

  • Create a website to log location and soil type

  • Within an area of 15m2, count the different species of plants and document

  • Compare number of species in each area

Equipment

  • Jar

  • PH probe

  • Small sieves

  • Measuring tape

  • Container

  • Filter paper
